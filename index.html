AI Chat Visibility FAQ

Clear answers for business owners who want to show up when people ask AI chats who to hire, trust or compare.

Can KRONATRIX guarantee that AI chats will recommend my business?

No. No ethical company can guarantee AI chat recommendations, rankings, citations, traffic, leads or sales. KRONATRIX improves the foundations that make a business clearer, more crawlable, better structured and easier to understand.

What does it mean to show up on AI chats?

It means your business is clear enough to be found, understood, compared or referenced when people ask AI systems for help. Visibility may come from your website, third-party mentions, structured data, useful answers and authority signals.

What is AI Chat Visibility?

AI Chat Visibility is the work of making a business easier for AI systems and humans to understand by improving the website entity, service clarity, proof, prompt-answer content, schema, speed and authority.

Why might my business not appear in AI answers?

Common reasons include vague service pages, weak proof, poor crawlability, missing structured data, unclear location, no useful FAQs, thin content, slow pages and weak external authority.

Is this the same as traditional SEO?

It overlaps with SEO but goes further. Traditional SEO focuses on search engines and users. AI.SEO also asks whether AI systems can extract the answer, understand the entity and compare the business with alternatives.

What should my website answer first?

It should answer who you are, what you do, who you serve, where you operate, why you can be trusted, what proof supports you and what the visitor should do next.

Does schema help AI chats?

Schema can help clarify entities, services, FAQs and page context. It is not a magic ranking trick and must match visible content. KRONATRIX uses JSON-LD honestly, without fake reviews or invisible claims.

What is llms.txt?

llms.txt is a plain text context file that summarises a site for AI systems and human reviewers. It is not a guarantee of visibility, but it can make important site context easier to access.

How fast can my site become more AI-readable?

Technical improvements can be made quickly, especially on a static site. Visibility in search or AI systems depends on crawling, indexing, authority, proof, competition and time.
