ASSET GUIDANCE

This KRONATRIX site uses flat root image paths only.

Required image files:
- logo.webp
- mascot.webp
- og-image.webp
- favicon-32.png
- favicon-64.png
- apple-touch-icon.png

Do not place images inside an assets folder.

Logo:
logo.webp is the KRONATRIX glowing K symbol.

Mascot:
mascot.webp is Spencer Aurelius, the KRONATRIX mascot.

Social preview:
og-image.webp is the Open Graph and Twitter/X preview image.

All files must remain in the same folder as index.html for GitHub upload.
